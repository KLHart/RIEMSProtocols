Abdominal Pain
Recognition
  • Patient with complaint of abdominal pain, discomfort or cramping

Treatment for all providers

  • Routine patient care
  • If the history and/or signs and symptoms are suggestive of a cardiac etiology, manage per the Chest Pain- Acute Coronary-Syndrome -STEMI Protocol.
  • Manage hypotension, poor perfusion, or shock per the age appropriate:
    • Adult General Shock and Hypotension Protocol
    • Pediatric General Shock and Hypotension Protocol
  • Transport the patient to the nearest appropiate hispital emergency facility
Cardiac/Advanced/Paramedic
  • Acquire a multi-lead ECG in any patient &ge 35 yo. Manage per the Chest Pain Acute Coronary-Syndrome-STEMI Protocol if findings suggest a cardiac etiology
  • Analgesia and antiemetic therapy as indicated per the age appropriate:
    • Adult Patient Comfort
    • Pediatric Patient Comfort
